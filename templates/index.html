The application has two modes:

[Sensor Mode]

In this mode, A Machine Learning model (Sensor Model) will give an evaluated score of the
task using the data from seneors all over the house.
The Sensor Model is trained by the data from the project.   (how it works)

Sensor Model Demo>>

[Manual Mode]

Under this mode, a worker (crowdsourcing worker or on-site staff) will watch the video of the task.
And give a score of how the participant finish the task.   (how it works)
Please note that the manual mode might need the participant to sign up of the project.
And the evaluate process might take up to 7 days to get the score from worker.
